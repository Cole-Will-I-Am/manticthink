# SEER Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

SEER ("the app") is provided by the SEER team ("we", "our", "us"). This policy
explains what data the app collects, how it is collected, how it is used, and the
third parties it is shared with. SEER is an AI chat client: to generate replies,
it must send the content of your conversations to an AI provider.

## Information We Collect and How

- **Messages and conversation content** — the text you type, the prior messages
  in a conversation, and any images or files you attach. Collected directly from
  your input when you send a message.
- **System instructions and tool definitions** — the system prompt and tool
  configuration used to process a request. Generated by the app for each request.
- **API credentials** — the API key(s) you enter (Ollama and, optionally,
  OpenAI). Collected when you enter them and stored on your device in the system
  Keychain. Credentials are sent only to their respective provider to
  authenticate your requests.
- **App settings and local chat history** — stored locally on your device.
- **Diagnostics** — limited error/performance diagnostics may be logged locally
  on the device to help diagnose problems. These are not transmitted to us.

We do not operate analytics or advertising trackers, and we do not maintain our
own server that receives your chat content.

## How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

- send your messages, attachments, and system instructions to your configured AI
  provider so it can generate a response;
- authenticate your requests using the API key you provide;
- save your preferences and conversation state on your device;
- diagnose crashes, errors, and performance issues locally.

## Third-Party AI Providers (Data Sharing)

When you send a message, your message content, conversation history, attachments,
and system instructions are transmitted to the AI provider you have configured:

- **Ollama Cloud** — the default provider, reached at `ollama.com`, operated by
  Ollama. Used for the built-in models.
- **OpenAI** — used only if you add an OpenAI API key, reached at
  `api.openai.com`, operated by OpenAI, for requests to OpenAI models.
- **A custom server** — if you configure a custom server address, your data is
  sent to that server instead of the default.

These providers process your data under their own privacy policies, which provide
their own protections for your information:

- Ollama: <https://ollama.com/privacy>
- OpenAI: <https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy>

We share your content with these providers only to fulfill your request to
generate a response. Before any chat content is sent, the app presents an in-app
disclosure identifying what data is sent and to which provider, and requires your
explicit agreement.

On macOS, optional Model Context Protocol (MCP) tools you enable run locally and
may, when you invoke them, access local resources or third-party services you
configure (for example a code repository or search API). Tool results may be
included in subsequent requests to the AI provider. You control which tools are
enabled.

## Data Storage and Retention

- Local chat history, settings, and credentials remain on your device unless you
  delete them or uninstall the app.
- Content sent to an AI provider is retained according to that provider's policy.
- We do not sell personal information.
- We do not use your content for advertising.

## Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards, including encrypted network transport
(HTTPS/TLS) for requests to providers and secure on-device storage (Keychain) for
credentials.

## Your Choices

You can:

- review the in-app data-sharing disclosure at any time in Settings → Privacy;
- read this privacy policy from Settings → Privacy;
- remove or replace your API credentials in Settings;
- delete conversations from within the app;
- uninstall the app to remove local app data.

If you do not agree to share data with an AI provider, the app cannot generate
responses; you may decline and uninstall the app.

## Children's Privacy

SEER is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
personal information from children under 13.
